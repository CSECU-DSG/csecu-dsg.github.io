    Natural Language Processing @ University of Chittagong

    Welcome to CSECU-DSG! We are the first-ever Data Science research group at the University of Chittagong

    Natural Language Processing (NLP) is the primary domain of our research, and this group led by Dr. Abu Nowshed Chy. Our group was founded in 2020, and we are proud to have won top honors in global competitions like CASE@EMNLP, CEREX@FIRE, and CLEF.

    We conduct active research in diverse NLP topics such as Information Extraction, Multimodal NLP, Multilingual and Low-resource NLP, Lexical Semantics, Computational Linguistics, and Natural Language Processing. Check our team members’ websites and publications to find out more!
